A tourist visa is a type of visa that allows individuals to enter a foreign country like France for the purpose of tourism and leisure activities. It is typically a short-term visa that grants permission for a specified duration, often ranging from a few weeks to several months, depending on the country issuing the visa

Tourist visas usually have restrictions that prevent the visa holder from engaging in any form of employment or business activities during their stay

The primary purpose is for the individual to visit the country for recreational purposes, such as sightseeing, visiting friends or family, attending cultural events, or participating in tourism-related activities

It's important for travelers to check the visa requirements of the France to visit well in advance of their intended travel dates to ensure a smooth and hassle-free entry process.
